# Welcome to Sample Flow Nexus

Sample Flow Nexus is MacLeather Co.'s comprehensive ERP system designed specifically for leather goods manufacturing. From initial sample creation through production, export, and delivery, Sample Flow Nexus streamlines every step of your workflow.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="End-to-End Sample Management">
    Track samples from initial design through customer approval. Upload reference files, manage material workflows, and seamlessly convert approved samples to production orders.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Production Tracking with QR Codes">
    Generate job cards with QR codes for real-time tracking on the factory floor. Workers can scan to log progress, and managers get instant visibility into order status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Comprehensive Export Documentation">
    Generate all required export documents including commercial invoices, packing lists, and shipping documentation. Calculate landed costs and manage carton planning efficiently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer-Specific Workflows">
    Pre-configured workflows for major customers like Boohoo, ASOS, and Next. Automatic label generation, customer-specific templates, and compliance with their requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
